Our Story

A simple beginning. A beautiful journey. 🌸

Where it all began ✨

Our story started in a random way. We first crossed paths on an app called Kiss Kiss.

At first, we didn't really talk much. Just small messages here and there — the kind you send without expecting anything in return.

But somehow we met again there. Maybe it was coincidence. Maybe it was a small, beautiful plan made by the universe.

♥ ♥ ♥

Slowly, the conversations became longer. What started as short replies turned into long exchanges — full of laughter, honesty, and warmth.

The laughs became real. The silences became comfortable. And the connection became something neither of us could quite explain.

"Sometimes the best stories begin when you least expect them — in the most unexpected places, with the most unexpected people."
A connection that grew 💕

And here we are — a story still being written, one message, one laugh, and one beautiful moment at a time.

Every small thing — a good morning text, a random meme, a voice note — all of it adds up to something worth keeping forever.
